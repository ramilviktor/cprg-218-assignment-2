Sneakers

A peak into sneaker culture

  • What's there to know?

    Being a sneakerhead means being passionate about collecting, wearing, and appreciating sneakers, often rare or limited edition ones. Sneakerheads often have deep knowledge about different brands, styles, and releases, and they enjoy the culture and community surrounding sneakers, including events, collaborations, and trading.

Sneakerheads

  • Jordans started off as basketball shoes but slowly turned into very sought after shoes.

  • Sneaker companies often collaborate with celebrities, athletes, and designers to create limited edition sneakers, driving hype and demand among collectors.

  • Sneaker collecting, or "sneakerhead" culture, gained mainstream popularity in the 1980s and has since become a global phenomenon.
